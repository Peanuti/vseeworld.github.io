Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Vseeworld

Is my video footage stored securely?

Yes. All video is encrypted and stored locally on your device or private cloud. We never access your data. Your privacy is our top priority and we comply with GDPR regulations.

Do I need a subscription?

Basic features are free. Cloud recording and advanced AI features require a low-cost plan starting at $4.99/month. We offer a 30-day free trial for premium features.

Can I use it without internet?

The camera works offline for local recording, but remote viewing and real-time alerts require internet connectivity. Local storage is available for offline use.

What devices are supported?

We support iOS 12+ and Android 8+.0+ devices. Our web dashboard is accessible from any modern browser. Compatible with most smart home systems.
